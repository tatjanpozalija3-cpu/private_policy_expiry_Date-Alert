What the App does

Expiry Date: Alert helps you track items with expiration dates (e.g., groceries, medicines, documents) and receive reminders before they expire.

Information we collect

We designed the App to work with minimal personal data. Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of information:

1) Information you provide

  • Item details you manually add (e.g., item name, category, expiration date, notes, photos you choose to attach).
  • Reminder settings you configure (e.g., notification timeframes, categories).

2) Device and app-level information

  • Device identifiers (e.g., a random identifier assigned by Apple to deliver notifications).
  • App diagnostics and performance (crash logs, error reports, basic usage events) when you opt in to share analytics or when your device settings permit it.

3) Optional cloud sync

If you enable iCloud or another platform sync feature, your item data may be stored in your personal cloud account under your control. Storage and processing are then also subject to the terms and privacy policy of that cloud provider (e.g., Apple iCloud).

No account required. The App does not require you to create an in‑app account. We do not collect your name, email, or phone number from within the App unless you contact us for support.

How we use information

  • Provide core App functionality (track items and schedule local notifications).
  • Customize reminders and preferences you set.
  • Improve safety, reliability, and performance (e.g., debugging and crash analysis).
  • Respond to support inquiries.

We do not sell your personal information.

Legal bases for processing (EEA/UK users)

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the App you requested.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep the App secure and reliable, and to understand app performance.
  • Consent: for optional analytics or notifications where required by law. You may withdraw consent at any time in your device settings or in‑app preferences (where available).

Retention

  • On-device data: stored until you delete the item in the App or uninstall the App.
  • Diagnostics/analytics: kept only as long as necessary for the purposes described above and as governed by our processors’ retention policies.

Sharing and disclosure

We may share data with:

  • Service providers/processors that help us operate the App (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, push notification delivery). They are bound by contractual confidentiality and data protection obligations and may only process data on our instructions.
  • Cloud providers (e.g., Apple iCloud) when you choose to enable sync.
  • Authorities when required to comply with law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not share item contents with advertisers.

International transfers

We may process and store information in countries outside your own. Where required, we implement safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses to protect your information.

  • Photos/Camera: The App will only access photos or camera if you grant permission; you can revoke it in device settings.
  • Access/Deletion: Delete items individually in the App or uninstall the App to remove on-device data. You can also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of data we hold as controller.
